Cajun Wings

Description

Cajun chicken wings offer a tantalizing explosion of flavors inspired by the vibrant culinary culture of Louisiana. Coated in a harmonious blend of Cajun spices, these wings boast a perfect balance of heat and depth. The seasoning, featuring paprika, cayenne pepper, and aromatic herbs, infuses the wings with a distinctive kick that's both rich and satisfying. Whether baked or fried, the wings acquire a crispy exterior that contrasts wonderfully with their succulent interior, creating a culinary experience that's as exciting as it is flavorful. Served with classic accompaniments like ranch or blue cheese dip, Cajun chicken wings are a staple at gatherings, parties, and casual feasts, where their bold taste takes center stage. These wings encapsulate the spirit of Cajun cuisine, inviting everyone to partake in a finger-licking journey through the heart of Louisiana's robust and lively flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 lb) bag chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon cayenne
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 1⁄4 cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Rinse chicken and pat dry.
  2. (if wings are whole, cut off and discard pointed tip and halve wings at the main joint.) In food processor or blender, combine all remaining ingredients, EXCEPT oil.
  3. Process until well blended.
  4. With machine on, slowly pour in oil and process until a thick paste forms.
  5. In a large bowl, combine wings and paste.
  6. Toss until wings are well coated.
  7. Cover and refrigerate over night.
  8. Broil or grill wings until nicely browned outside and cooked through.
  9. Serve warm or at room temperature.
