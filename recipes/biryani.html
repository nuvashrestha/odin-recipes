Chicken Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent but is now enjoyed worldwide. It is typically made by layering fragrant, long-grain rice with marinated meat (such as chicken, lamb, or beef) and a blend of spices, including cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and saffron. The dish is often cooked slowly, allowing the flavors to meld together and the meat to become tender. Variations of biryani abound, with different regions and cultures adding their unique twists, such as using seafood, vegetables, or even eggs as the main ingredient. Biryani is known for its rich taste, combining the earthy flavors of rice and meat with the aromatic and pungent spices, making it a beloved and popular dish for special occasions and everyday meals alike.

Ingredients for Chicken Marination

  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1 1/4 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon biryani masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 6 ounces boneless skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (about 8 cups)

Steps for marinating the chicken

  1. Make few slits on all the chicken pieces and add to a large bowl. Then add all of the ingredients above.
  2. Mix everything well and marinate the chicken. Cover it and let it aside for an hour.
  3. While the chicken is marinating, add 2 cups of basmati rice to a large pot and rinse it till the water is almost clear. Drain and soak in fresh water for 30 minutes. Drain to a colander after 30 minutes.

Ingredients for Cooking Chicken

  • 2 or 3 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 6 cloves
  • 1 small cinnamon piece
  • 1 star anise
  • 3/4 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 2 or 3 spoons ghee (butter)
  • 1 strand mace
  • half of an onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon biryani masala
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3 teaspoon red chilli powder (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 or 3 green thai chilli (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped mint leaves

Steps for cooking the chicken

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a bottom pot or pressure cooker. Then add all of the spices into the pan. Wait to add other ingredients.
  2. Add the onions. On a medium heat, fry them while stirring often till the onions are light brown.
  3. Add marinated chicekn and saute till it becomes pale for 5 minutes.
  4. Lower the flame then cover and cook until the chicken is soft, tender and completely cooked.
  5. Make sure chicken is just cooked and not overcooked. Evaporate any excess moisture left in the pot by cooking further without the lid.
  6. Taste test and add more salt if needed. Then add the rest of the ingredients listed under 'Ingredients for Cooking Chicken' if needed.

Ingredients for Making Chicken Briyani

  • 3 cups water
  • salt to taste

Steps for making chicken biryani

  1. Mix everything well. Spread it evenly in a single layer.
  2. Layer drained rice over the chicken.
  3. Level the rice gently on top. Add mint leaves. Do no mix everything.
  4. Finally cook the cooker.
  5. Cook until you hear 1 whistle. Later remove the cooker from hot burner to stop cooking further.
  6. When the pressuer releases naturally, open the lid and gently fluff up with a fork. Serve the chicken biryani and enjoy!
